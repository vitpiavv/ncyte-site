Governance & assurance

Compliance consultancy

We help organizations align security practices with industry frameworks and regulations—so controls are defensible under audit and useful day to day.

Whether you are preparing for an assessment, implementing a control baseline, or maturing a compliance program, we provide structured guidance and hands-on support. Our consultants bridge the gap between policy language and operational reality.

Frameworks we support

Common engagements include SOC 2 (Trust Services Criteria), ISO 27001, GDPR-aligned privacy programs, and sector-specific requirements. We prioritize evidence collection, risk treatment, and sustainable processes—not checkbox paperwork alone.

Contact us to discuss scope, timelines, and how compliance consultancy fits alongside our training and mentoring services.
